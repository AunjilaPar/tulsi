About TULSI

Created just off the Market at Carmody Street.Ennis Tulsi has set the standard for authentic Indian Cuisine.Tulsi is centrally located so it's easy to park up at the market and walk over

Tulsi has great décor with a nice modern yet Indian touch to it.India's gastronomic fare devrives its rich history from a 4,000 year timeline so there is much more to Indian cuisine than the spicy curries.Tulsi has a range of mild,medium and hot dishes.

If there is nothing that sparks your interest on the menu the Tulsi's magnificent chef can cook you up whatever you want.On the other hand if there are too many tasty dishes to choose from why not try the Tulsi special and have a tasting of several appetizers and mains.
